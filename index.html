About Me

I am currently working as an Assistant Professor in the Department of Information Technology at the National Institute of Technology Karnataka (NITK), Surathkal. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Applications from the National Institute of Technology Tiruchirappalli (NIT Trichy) and my M.Tech. in Computer Science from the University of Hyderabad. Prior to joining NITK, I served as an Assistant Professor at GITAM University, Hyderabad, and SR University, Warangal.
